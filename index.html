Solid waste statistics



Do you know which type of solid waste disposed at the landfills most?

From the pie chart below, we can observe that the landfills are mainly constituted by municipal solid waste during 2009 to 2015.



Let's take a look at the compositions of municipal solid waste of past 7 years.

Domestic waste is the most and the maximum amount of domestic waste is 6460 tonnes per day.



Futher look at the compositions of municipal solid waste in 2015.

In the category of municipal solid waste,the portion of domestic waste is greater than commercial and industrial waste.



From the above graph, we know that most of the waste are domestic waste, which covers post-consumer residential waste and refuse collected in public area.

For the sake to protect the enviornment, we need to reduce waste on a daily basis.
